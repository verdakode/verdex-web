How Verdex Works

Verdex is a revolutionary app connecting local 3D printer owners with people who need items printed. Here's how it works:

  • Designers upload 3D models to our marketplace
  • Buyers browse and purchase designs
  • Local 3D printer owners (our "Makers") receive print jobs
  • Makers print and deliver items, often within 24 hours
